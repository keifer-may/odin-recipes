White Cheddar Mac and Cheese



Description

Another pasta favorite in the Harman-May household. I don't care how old you are, this dish is the perfect side or the perfect entree. Stick to your ribs good.

Ingredients

  • 16 ounces uncooked elbow macaroni
  • 2 ½ cups milk
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 teaspoon garlic salt
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ cup margarine
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 ½ cups shredded white Cheddar cheese
  • ½ cup shredded Parmesan cheese, divided
  • ¼ cup panko bread crumbs
  • ¼ cup real bacon bits
  • 1 tablespoon snipped chives
